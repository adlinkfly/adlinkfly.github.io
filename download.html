How To Download

2. If an Error page displays, simply refresh or reload the page.

3. Enter the password in the box to get the download link.

4. Click the 下载 button to start your download.

5. If download option box displays, click the 普通下载 button for a regular download. You may need to log in with your Baidu, QQ or Weibo account.
